Data Management

Because Polly stores almost everything locally, most of these actions take effect immediately on your device. Use the options below to export, edit, or permanently delete your data.

How this works: Polly doesn't require an account, and your photo library, chat history, and app stats live on your device, not on our servers. The requests below are handled by our support team and, where an action can only be completed on-device, we'll point you to the exact place in the app to do it.

Request a data export

Get a copy of any information we hold about you (such as past support conversations). Most in-app stats can be viewed directly inside Polly under Settings → Your Stats.

Edit your data

Update the information Polly has stored, including your support contact details or in-app preferences. In-app stats and settings are editable directly from the app.

Revoke consent

Withdraw consent for optional data uses, such as sharing crash and performance diagnostics with the developer. You can also manage this anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

Delete account & data

Permanently delete any data associated with you, including support history. This does not delete your photo library — that remains fully under your control on your device.

Removing local data yourself

Since most Polly data never leaves your device, you can wipe it immediately without waiting on a request:

  • Open Settings → Polly → Reset Stats to clear Hunger, Education, and Fun progress.
  • Delete the Polly app from your device to remove all locally stored app data, including quiz history and chat logs.
  • Photos deleted through Polly follow standard iOS deletion — they move to "Recently Deleted" before permanent removal.

Delete your account & data?

This permanently removes any data we hold about you (such as support history) within 30 days. This action can't be undone. Your on-device photo library and app stats are not affected — manage those directly in the app.
