Features

Three habits, one companion.

Polly's mood is a mirror of three simple habits. Feed one and watch the needle move — literally.

Clean your photo library

Swipe through your camera roll like a deck of cards. Right to delete, left to keep. Every file removed is real storage freed — and a real, calculated CO₂ saving.

Learn about data pollution

Short quiz questions unpack the hidden environmental cost of digital data — from standby power draw to the energy behind a single cloud backup.

Chat with Polly, anytime

Ask about your digital footprint or how to shrink it. Polly runs on Apple Intelligence, fully on-device — your conversations never leave your phone.

How it works

A one-minute ritual, not a chore.

No setup, no sign-in. Open the app, pick a stat to raise, and Polly does the rest.

STEP 1

Open Polly

Polly greets you and shows exactly which stat is lowest — Hunger, Education, or Fun — so you always know what to do next.

STEP 2

Pick an action

Swipe through photos to delete clutter, answer a quick quiz question, or chat with Polly about your digital footprint.

STEP 3

Watch the impact

Every action logs real numbers — megabytes freed, grams of CO₂ saved — while Polly's mood visibly improves.
