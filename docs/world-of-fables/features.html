The Features

Discover all the mechanics designed to educate and entertain.

Interactive Map

A touch-guided mascot with walking animations. Move freely through the clouds and discover the visual paths to unlock the chapters of the fable.

Sequencing Game

Drag the story cards into the correct narrative order to unlock the event. Orchestral audio feedback rewards exact moves with new progressive musical layers.

Augmented Reality Book

Complete all events to unlock the final ARKit book, featuring custom shaders for page bending in the real world.
