World of Fables is designed from the ground up with accessibility and inclusivity at its core. Because the magic of fables should be experienced by all children, without barriers.

Inclusive Design

  • OpenDyslexic Font: A dedicated toggle allows you to instantly activate the OpenDyslexic font to facilitate reading.
  • Spoken Narration: Native VoiceOver support and an in-app option to read all dialogues aloud.
  • Contrast and Colour: Full WCAG compliance. Support for increasing or reducing contrast. Furthermore, we distinguish elements without ever relying solely on colour, using dedicated visual patterns for colour blindness.
  • Reduce Motion: Animations sync with system settings to reduce motion, helping more sensitive children.
  • Math Gate: All advanced settings are protected by a math quiz, preventing accidental changes by children.
  • Dynamic Type: All fonts scale natively with your iPad preferences.
