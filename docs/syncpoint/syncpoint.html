The app

Your personal umpire, on your wrist and in your pocket.

SyncPoint tracks the match point by point: you focus on playing, it keeps score, service, court changes and stats. On court with Apple Watch, on the sidelines with iPhone.

Score from your wrist

Two large buttons, distinct haptics for point, game and set, undo with the Digital Crown and Double Tap to score without touching the screen.

Giant scoreboard on iPhone

Court-style scoreboard visible from afar. Full game engine: works without a Watch too, with point editing and undo from your phone.

Live Activity & Dynamic Island

Real-time scoreboard on the Lock Screen: anyone off court can follow the match at a glance, without opening the app.

Official workout

Every match is a workout in Apple Health: calories, live heart rate and Activity Rings closing while you play.

Stats & head-to-head

Filterable history, win rate against each opponent, doubles pair analysis and match momentum chart, point by point.

Wins worth sharing

Graphic result cards in Story and Post format, customizable with your photos. With shared matches, the result lands in every player’s history.
