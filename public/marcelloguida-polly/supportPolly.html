Common issues

Quick fixes before you write in.

Chat isn't responding

On-device AI chat requires Apple Intelligence, available on supported devices running a compatible iOS version. Check Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri to confirm it's enabled.

Photos won't load

Polly needs Photos access to scan your library. Check Settings → Polly → Photos and set it to "All Photos" or "Selected Photos."

Stats reset unexpectedly

Hunger, Education, and Fun stats decay slowly over time to reflect an idle library. This is expected behavior, not a bug — open Polly regularly to keep them topped up.
