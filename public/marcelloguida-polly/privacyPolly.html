Privacy Policy

Polly is built to work almost entirely on your device. This policy explains, in plain language, exactly what that means for your data.

1. Information we collect

Polly is designed to minimize what ever leaves your device. Here's a full breakdown:

Data typeCollected?Leaves your device?
Photo library contentsAccessed locally to display & deleteNever
Chat messages with PollyProcessed by on-device Apple IntelligenceNever
App stats (Hunger, Education, Fun)Stored locally on your deviceNever
Quiz answers & scoreStored locally on your deviceNever
Crash & performance diagnosticsOptional, if you enable sharing with developers in iOS SettingsOnly if enabled by you at the OS level
Support email contentsOnly if you email us or use the contact formSent to our support inbox

Polly does not require an account, so we do not collect names, phone numbers, or login credentials through the app itself. If you contact support, we receive whatever you choose to include in that message (e.g. your name and email address).

2. How we use information

Because nearly all app data stays on-device, "use" mostly happens locally, inside the app, to power its own features:

  • Displaying and letting you delete photos you choose to remove.
  • Calculating storage freed and estimated CO₂ saved from your actions.
  • Tracking your Hunger, Education, and Fun stats to reflect your activity.
  • Powering on-device AI chat responses about data pollution topics.
  • Responding to support requests you send us directly.

We do not use your photo library or chat content for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the app on your device.

3. How we store information

App data (photo library access, stats, quiz progress, chat history) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms and is protected by your device's own security (passcode, Face ID/Touch ID, encryption at rest). We do not operate a remote database of your in-app activity. Messages you send us through the support form or email are stored in our support mailbox for as long as needed to resolve your request and to meet reasonable record-keeping obligations, after which they are deleted or anonymized.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your photo library, chat content, or in-app stats with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties, because that data never reaches our servers in the first place. The limited exceptions are:

  • Apple, Inc. — the on-device AI chat runs through Apple Intelligence frameworks provided by iOS itself.
  • Support requests — information you voluntarily send us (e.g. via email or the contact form) is handled by our small support team and, where applicable, the email service provider that operates our inbox.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Polly, our users, or the public.

5. Cookies & tracking

The Polly mobile app does not use cookies or third-party advertising trackers. This website uses only strictly necessary, first-party technical storage needed to remember basic preferences (such as whether you've closed a notice) — no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are set.

6. Security

Because your photo library, chat history, and app stats stay on your device, they benefit from Apple's device-level protections — encryption at rest, secure enclave-backed authentication, and iOS app sandboxing. Any information you do send us (such as a support email) is transmitted over encrypted connections and access is limited to team members who need it to help you.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete personal information, and to withdraw consent for its processing. Because most Polly data lives only on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly by using the app or your device's settings — for example, deleting the app removes its locally stored data. For anything else, including requests related to information you've sent our support team, visit our Data Management page or contact us using the details below.

8. Children's privacy

Polly is not directed at children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your region), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

9. Contact us about privacy

Questions about this policy or how Polly handles data can be sent to mguida2604@gmail.com. You can also reach general support through our Support page.

10. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the app, legal requirements, or our practices. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page, and where appropriate, we'll provide additional notice (such as an in-app message) before the change takes effect.
