Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

World of Fables was built with children's privacy as its highest priority. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect and how it is protected.


1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, location data, or usage metrics. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an account to play.


2. In-App Purchases and Ads

World of Fables contains NO in-app purchases and NO advertisements. We believe in providing a safe, uninterrupted educational environment.


3. Parental Controls

Any settings or external links are hidden behind a Math Gate, ensuring that only parents and teachers can access them.


4. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please use the Contact page to reach out.
